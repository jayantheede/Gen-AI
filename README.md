# What is Human Intelligence?
 Human intelligence is the complex mental capacity for learning, reasoning, problem-solving, abstract thinking, and adapting to new situations, involving cognitive functions like perception, memory, and language to understand and control one's environment 
 # core Concepts: 

# Learning & Memory
Ability to acquire knowledge from experience, remember it, and use it later.

# Reasoning & Problem-Solving
Thinking logically, analyzing situations, and finding solutions to new or complex problems.

# Creativity
Generating new ideas, imagining possibilities, and thinking beyond fixed rules.

# Emotional Intelligence
Understanding emotions—your own and others’—and using empathy, self-control, and social skills effectively.

# Adaptability
Adjusting behavior and thinking in response to changing environments or unexpected situations.

# Language & Communication
Understanding, producing, and using language to express ideas and share meaning.

# Consciousness & Self-Awareness
Awareness of oneself, one’s thoughts, intentions, and existence.
